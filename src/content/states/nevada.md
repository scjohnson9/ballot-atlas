summary: Nevada's 2026 ballot includes Question 6, a proposed constitutional amendment that would establish a fundamental right to abortion up to fetal viability. Because Nevada requires initiated amendments to pass in two consecutive general elections, this is voters' second decision on the measure following its 2024 approval. The questions raised include the personhood of the unborn and the proper scope of state constitutional protection.
